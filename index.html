Modus Operandi

In a comprehensive study published on Monday, December 8,2020 , scientists estimated that about 385 million people, particularly among farmers and agriculture workers, are poisoned by pesticides every year including 11,000 deaths per year. Among the fatalities, nearly 60% or 6,600 deaths per year occur in India. This means 44% of the worldwide farming population (total 860 million) are poisoned by pesticides every year. “Beyond the immediate deaths, most people suffer long term chronic illnesses including neurological disorders. In India, during our surveys, we find that many farmers are not even aware of their chronic conditions caused by the pesticides.

How We Do This

  • How we are protecting the ecosystem ?

    We are trying to keep a balanced ecosystem not by killing the pests. In case if the pesticide are compulsorily required, then we will try to make usage of permissible amount pesticides.

  • See the how it Benefits to Farmers.

    As per the article titled “The global distribution of acute unintentional pesticide poisoning: estimations based on a systematic review” in BMC Public Health, revealed that about 44% of the worldwide farming population (total 860 million) are poisoned by pesticides every year. Regular and periodic monitoring of the crop life cycle prevents the use of pesticides beyond the permissible levels. Providing an alternative to killing of pests could be a direct solution to the problem.

  • See how it benefits the whole human Population.

    By periodical monitoring there is no excessive use of inorganic compounds that are toxic to human and harmful to the ecosystem Thus by we are creating healthier generation by removing Pesticides.

  • See how Farmers are getting more Profit.

    The combination of artificial intelligence and remote sensing technologies to monitor the life cycle of a crop and prevent the attack of pests. The system also creates a feasible environment for the healthy growth of the plant to increase productivity. Also we are providing an eComerce portal for products to sell from home there by avoiding the storage for a longer period.
